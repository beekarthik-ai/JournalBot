Journal Bot is a simple web app that tracks your mood based on journal entries. Journaling is a great method to deal with the stresses of life and Journal Bot aims to point out that the "lows" of life are often far shorter than they may seem.

If you are having serious mental health issues, please seek real medical help, Journal Bot is merely an assistive tool. Below are some resources.

National Suicide Hotline: 1-800-273-8255

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Association: 1-800-662-4357
